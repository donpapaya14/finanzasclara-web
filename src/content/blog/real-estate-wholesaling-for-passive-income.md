## Introduction to Real Estate Wholesaling for Passive Income
Real estate wholesaling for passive income is a strategy that involves acting as a middleman between a seller and a buyer, earning a profit without directly owning the property. According to the National Association of Realtors (NAR) 2022 Report, **real estate investing** can provide significant returns, with the median existing single-family home price increasing by **5.1%** in 2022. 

## What is Real Estate Wholesaling?
Real estate wholesaling differs from **flipping houses**, as it does not require renovating or holding onto properties. The benefits of wholesaling include low upfront costs and the potential for quick profits. As stated in the National Association of Realtors (NAR) 2022 Report, **wholesaling** can be an attractive option for those looking to get started in real estate investing.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Real Estate Wholesaling
To get started with wholesaling, follow these steps:
1. **Find motivated sellers**: Look for properties with **distressed owners** or those in **pre-foreclosure**.
2. **Negotiate a purchase price**: Offer a price that is **10-20%** below market value.
3. **Assign the contract**: Find a buyer and assign the contract for a **fee**, typically **$5,000-$10,000**. 
As outlined in Real Estate Investing for Dummies, 4th Edition by Eric Tyson and Robert S. Griswold, a thorough understanding of the process is crucial for success.
