## Introduction to Hidden Subscriptions
**Hidden subscription costs** can add up quickly, with the average person losing hundreds of dollars per year to unwanted recurring charges. According to a study by the [American Customer Satisfaction Index (ACSI)](https://www.theacsi.org), 2022, **62% of consumers** have experienced unwanted subscription charges. 

## Common Hidden Subscriptions to Watch Out For
Examples of popular services like **streaming platforms**, **software trials**, and **free trials** that can lead to recurring charges are common. A study by the [American Customer Satisfaction Index (ACSI)](https://www.theacsi.org) 2022 found that **45% of consumers** have been charged for a subscription they did not want.

## Identifying Hidden Subscriptions on Your Bank Statements
To identify hidden subscriptions, review your bank statements regularly. The [Federal Trade Commission (FTC)](https://www.ftc.gov) recommends checking your statements for recurring charges you do not recognize. 

## Cancelling Subscriptions: A Step-by-Step Guide
To cancel a subscription, follow these steps:
1. **Log in** to your account
2. **Find the subscription** you want to cancel
3. **Click on the cancel button**
4. **Confirm** you want to cancel

The [Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)](https://www.consumerfinance.gov) provides a guide on cancelling recurring payments.

## Avoiding Hidden Subscriptions in the Future
To avoid hidden subscriptions, **opt out of free trials** and **cancel subscriptions** before they auto-renew. [NerdWallet](https://www.nerdwallet.com) recommends setting reminders to cancel subscriptions before they renew.
