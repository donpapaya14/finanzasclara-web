## Banks' Sneakiest Overdraft Triggers
Banks use transaction reordering to process large charges first, causing multiple fees. For example, [Consumer Reports investigation 2023](https://www.consumerreports.org/) found that some banks prioritize debit card transactions over bill payments, leading to unnecessary overdraft fees.

## Opt-out Loophole Nobody Uses
To disable overdraft 'protection' via online banking/app, follow these steps:
1. Log in to your online banking account
2. Go to account settings
3. Look for 'overdraft protection' or 'overdraft transfer'
4. Select 'opt-out' or 'disable'
As stated in the [FDIC Regulation E guidelines](https://www.fdic.gov/), consumers have the right to opt-out of overdraft protection.
