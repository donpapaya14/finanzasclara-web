## What is Mint and How Does it Work?
Mint's features include account linking, transaction categorization, and budgeting tools. As stated on [Mint.com](https://www.mint.com), users can link their bank accounts, credit cards, and investments to track their spending. ## Setting Up Mint: A Step-by-Step Guide
To set up Mint, follow these steps:
1. Download the Mint app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Create an account and link your bank accounts, credit cards, and investments.
3. Set up budget categories and configure notifications.
As per [Mint Support](https://support.mint.com), users can also set up bill tracking and payment reminders.
