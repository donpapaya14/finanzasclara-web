## Introduction to Spendee
The **Spendee budgeting app** is a powerful tool for managing personal finances, offering automated expense tracking and budgeting features. According to the [Spendee Official Website](https://www.spendee.com), it helps users track their spending across multiple accounts and set financial goals. With **free money management apps** like Spendee, individuals can take control of their financial lives.

## Setting Up Spendee Step-by-Step
To get started with Spendee, follow these steps:
1. Download the Spendee app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Create an account or log in if you already have one.
3. Connect your bank accounts and credit cards to Spendee.
As outlined in the [Spendee Support Documentation](https://support.spendee.com), this process is straightforward and secure.

## Linking Bank Accounts to Spendee
Linking your bank accounts to Spendee allows for automatic transaction tracking. According to the [Spendee Security Policy](https://www.spendee.com/security), all data is encrypted and protected. To link an account:
1. Go to the Spendee dashboard.
2. Click on 'Add Account'.
3. Select your bank from the list of supported institutions.
This feature is comparable to what is offered by **Spendee vs Mint**, another popular budgeting tool.

## Creating a Budget with Spendee
Creating a budget with Spendee involves setting financial goals and allocating funds accordingly. As advised by [The Balance](https://www.thebalance.com), a key step is to track your income and expenses to understand where your money is going. Then, you can set realistic targets for savings and spending.

## Using Spendee for Investment Tracking
Spendee can also be used to monitor investments, helping you stay on top of your financial portfolio. According to [Investopedia](https://www.investopedia.com), tracking investments is crucial for making informed financial decisions. With Spendee, you can link your investment accounts and view your portfolio performance alongside your everyday spending.

## Spendee Premium Features and Subscription
Spendee offers premium features, including advanced budgeting and investment tracking, for a subscription fee. As detailed on the [Spendee Pricing Page](https://www.spendee.com/pricing), these features provide additional insights and tools for managing your finances. The premium subscription includes:
* Advanced budgeting categories
* Investment tracking with real-time updates
* Priority customer support
