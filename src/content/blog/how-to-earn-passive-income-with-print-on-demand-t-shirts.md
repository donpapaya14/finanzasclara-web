## Introduction to Print on Demand Passive Income
To earn **print on demand passive income**, consider starting a **t-shirt business** with no upfront cost. According to the Shopify Print-on-Demand Report 2023, this model allows for the creation and sale of products without holding any inventory, making it an ideal **side hustle**.

## Why print-on-demand is the best zero-cost side hustle
Print-on-demand services produce and ship products only after they have been sold, eliminating the need for inventory storage and upfront costs. As reported by Shopify, this approach reduces the financial risk associated with starting an online business. With **print on demand**, entrepreneurs can focus on designing and marketing their products without worrying about production and logistics.

## Where to sell your shirts for maximum exposure
To maximize exposure, consider selling your shirts on marketplaces like Etsy and Amazon Merch. Alternatively, create a standalone store using Shopify or WooCommerce. As reported by [Amazon Merch by Amazon 2023 Seller Data](https://www.amazon.com), Amazon Merch offers a vast customer base and easy integration with Amazon services. When selling on these platforms, focus on optimizing your product listings with relevant keywords and high-quality images.

## Realistic earnings: What to expect month 1 vs month 6
According to the Print-on-Demand Income Survey 2023, realistic earnings for month 1 can range from $100 to $500, while month 6 can see earnings between $1,000 to $5,000. To scale beyond t-shirts, consider expanding your product line to include other print-on-demand items like mugs, phone cases, and tote bags.

## The one mistake that kills 90% of new POD sellers
To avoid copyright issues and trademark violations, ensure that your designs do not infringe on existing trademarks. As reported by the [USPTO Trademark Infringement Cases 2022](https://www.uspto.gov), copyright infringement can result in account bans and financial losses. Research your designs thoroughly and use public domain images or purchase licenses for copyrighted materials.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How much does it cost to start a print-on-demand business?
Starting a print-on-demand business can cost as little as $0, as many platforms offer free sign-up and no upfront costs. However, investing in design software like Canva or Adobe Creative Cloud can range from $10 to $50 per month.
### What are the most popular print-on-demand products?
According to the Printful 2023 Report, the most popular print-on-demand products include t-shirts, hoodies, and phone cases. Consider offering a variety of products to cater to different customer preferences.
### How do I market my print-on-demand products?
To market your print-on-demand products, utilize social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook. Share high-quality product images and engage with your target audience to build brand awareness and drive sales.
### Can I sell print-on-demand products on Amazon?
Yes, you can sell print-on-demand products on Amazon using Amazon Merch. This platform allows you to create and sell custom products without holding any inventory, making it an ideal option for print-on-demand sellers.
### How do I track my print-on-demand sales and profits?
To track your print-on-demand sales and profits, use analytics tools provided by your print-on-demand platform or e-commerce software. Monitor your sales, profits, and customer demographics to optimize your marketing strategies and improve your business performance.
### What are the benefits of using print-on-demand services?
The benefits of using print-on-demand services include no upfront costs, no inventory risk, and the ability to focus on designing and marketing products. According to the Shopify Print-on-Demand Report 2023, print-on-demand services can help entrepreneurs start and grow a successful online business.
