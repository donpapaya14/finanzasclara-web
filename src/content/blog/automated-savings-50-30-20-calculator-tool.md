## The 50-30-20 Budgeting Rule: A Simple Yet Effective Savings Strategy
The **50-30-20 budgeting rule** is a simple yet effective savings strategy that can help you allocate your income towards necessities, discretionary spending, and savings. According to the [National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC)](https://www.nfcc.org/), this rule can help you achieve financial stability. Here's how to apply this rule:
* **50% for necessities**: Allocate 50% of your income towards necessities such as rent, utilities, and groceries.
* **30% for discretionary spending**: Allocate 30% of your income towards discretionary spending such as entertainment, hobbies, and travel.
* **20% for savings**: Allocate 20% of your income towards savings and debt repayment.
