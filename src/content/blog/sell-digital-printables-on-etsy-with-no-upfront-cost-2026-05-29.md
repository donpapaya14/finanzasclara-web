## Introduction to Selling Digital Printables on Etsy
To **sell digital printables on Etsy** with no upfront cost, you first need to understand what digital printables are and why they sell. Digital printables are downloadable files that customers can print at home, such as planners, wall art, and stickers. According to the [Etsy 2023 Seller Trends Report](https://www.etsy.com), the demand for digital products has increased significantly, with over **70%** of sellers reporting an increase in sales.

Digital printables are popular due to their convenience and customization options. The most popular categories include:

To set up an Etsy shop for free, follow these steps:
