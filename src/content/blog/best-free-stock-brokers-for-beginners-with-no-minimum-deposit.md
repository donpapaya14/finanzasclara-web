### Can I start investing with $100?
Yes, you can start investing with **$100**. Many brokers, including Robinhood and Webull, offer **fractional shares**, allowing you to invest in stocks with as little as $1.

### What is payment for order flow?
**Payment for order flow (PFOF)** refers to the practice of brokers selling their customers' orders to market makers or other firms. This can affect execution prices and is a crucial factor to consider when choosing a broker.

### How do I choose the best broker for me?
To choose the best broker for you, consider your **investment goals**, **risk tolerance**, and **trading frequency**. You can also consult the [SEC's Investor.gov](https://www.investor.gov/) website for more information.
