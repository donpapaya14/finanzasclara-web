## What are digital printables and why they sell
Digital printables are downloadable, printable files that customers can purchase and print at home. According to the **Etsy 2023 Seller Trends Report**, popular categories include planners, wall art, and stickers. The market demand for digital printables on Etsy is high, with **75% of sellers reporting an increase in sales**.

## Free tools to design printables (no Photoshop needed)
To design digital printables without Photoshop, you can use free tools like **Canva**, **Inkscape**, and **Google Slides**. Canva Design School 2024 offers beginner-friendly tutorials and templates to get you started. Here are some free design tools:
* Canva: A graphic design platform with a wide range of templates and design tools.
* Inkscape: A free and open-source vector graphics editor.
* Google Slides: A presentation software that can also be used for designing printables.

## How to set up an Etsy shop for free in 30 minutes
To set up an Etsy shop for free, follow these steps:
1. Create an Etsy account and click on "Sell on Etsy".
2. Choose your shop name and profile picture.
3. Set up your shop's appearance and add a profile description.
According to the **Etsy Seller Handbook 2023**, optimizing your listings with SEO is crucial to increase visibility.

## Zero-cost marketing strategies to get first sales
To drive traffic to your Etsy shop without ads, leverage **Pinterest**, **Instagram Reels**, and free **Facebook groups**. According to **HubSpot Social Media Trends 2024**, Instagram Reels have a higher engagement rate than regular posts. Here are some zero-cost marketing strategies:
* Share high-quality product images on Pinterest and Instagram.
* Utilize relevant hashtags to increase visibility.
* Participate in Facebook groups related to your niche.
