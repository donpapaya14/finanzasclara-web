### What are digital printables and why they sell

Digital printables are a type of digital product that can be downloaded and printed by customers. According to the Etsy 2023 Seller Trends Report, digital printables are one of the fastest-growing categories on the platform, with sales increasing by 25% in the past year. This is because digital printables offer a convenient and cost-effective solution for customers who want to create custom products without the need for inventory or shipping.

**Why digital printables require no upfront cost**: Unlike physical products, digital printables do not require any upfront costs for inventory or shipping. This makes them an attractive option for sellers who want to start selling without any initial investment.

### How to list printables on Etsy for free (step-by-step)

Listing digital printables on Etsy is a straightforward process that can be completed for free. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Create an Etsy account and set up your shop.
2. Choose a digital printable template from Canva or Google Slides.
3. Add a title and description to your printable.
4. Set a price for your printable (you can start with $0.99).
5. Use mockup generators like Placeit or Creative Market to create a high-quality image of your printable.
6. Add your printable to your Etsy shop and set it as a digital download.

### Scaling up: From 1 product to a passive income stream

To scale up your digital printable business, you'll need to create multiple products and automate your delivery process. Here are some tips:

* Batch-create printables: Use Canva or Google Slides to create multiple printables at once.
* Use Gumroad to automate delivery: Gumroad is a platform that allows you to sell digital products and automate delivery.
* Cross-promote on Pinterest: Use Pinterest to promote your digital printables and reach a wider audience.
