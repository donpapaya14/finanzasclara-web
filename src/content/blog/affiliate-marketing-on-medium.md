## Introduction to Medium Affiliate Marketing
Medium's partner program offers a unique opportunity for writers to earn **passive income** through **affiliate marketing**. According to the [Medium Partner Program Guidelines 2024](https://medium.com/partners), the program allows writers to earn money based on the engagement their articles receive. To get started with **Medium affiliate marketing**, it's essential to understand the basics of affiliate marketing, including how to choose profitable niche topics and set up affiliate links.

## Choosing Profitable Niche Topics
To succeed in **passive income writing**, research methods for selecting high-demand topics with affiliate potential are crucial. Using tools like [Google Trends 2024](https://trends.google.com), writers can identify trending topics and create content around them. For example, a study by [Pew Research Center](https://www.pewresearch.org) found that **73% of adults in the US** use the internet to learn about health and wellness, making it a profitable niche for affiliate marketing.

## Setting Up Affiliate Links on Medium
Setting up affiliate links on Medium requires a step-by-step approach. First, writers need to join an affiliate program like the [Amazon Associates Program](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com). Then, they can integrate affiliate links into their Medium articles using the **Amazon Associates Program Terms 2024** guidelines. Here are the steps to follow:
1. Create an Amazon Associates account
2. Choose a product to promote
3. Generate an affiliate link
4. Integrate the link into your Medium article

## Optimizing Articles for Affiliate Earnings
To increase affiliate link clicks and conversions, writers need to optimize their articles using **SEO and content strategies**. According to the [Moz SEO Guide 2024](https://moz.com/learn/seo), using relevant keywords, meta descriptions, and optimizing images can improve article visibility. Additionally, writers can use tools like [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com) to analyze their competition and create better content.

## Disclosing Affiliate Relationships
Compliance with **FTC guidelines** for affiliate marketing transparency is essential. According to the [FTC Affiliate Marketing Guidelines 2024](https://www.ftc.gov), writers must clearly disclose their affiliate relationships with readers. Here are some tips to follow:
* Use clear and concise language
* Disclose affiliate relationships at the beginning of the article
* Use affiliate links only when relevant to the content

## Tracking and Scaling Affiliate Income
To monitor affiliate earnings and expand the reach of Medium articles, writers can use tools like [Google Analytics Affiliate Tracking 2024](https://analytics.google.com). Here are some steps to follow:
1. Set up Google Analytics on your Medium account
2. Track affiliate link clicks and conversions
3. Analyze data to optimize content and increase earnings
