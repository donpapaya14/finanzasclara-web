## Avoid These 3 Common Couples’ App Mistakes
When using a couples’ finance app, it’s essential to avoid common mistakes. According to the [NerdWallet Couples Finance Report, 2023](https://www.nerdwallet.com), these include over-linking accounts, ignoring transaction notes, and not setting a monthly ‘money date’ to review finances.

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. **Over-linking accounts**: This can pose a privacy risk if the account is accessed by an unauthorized party.
2. **Ignoring transaction notes**: Notes can help clarify unclear transactions and prevent misunderstandings.
3. **Not setting a monthly ‘money date’**: Regularly reviewing finances together can help couples stay on track with their financial goals.
