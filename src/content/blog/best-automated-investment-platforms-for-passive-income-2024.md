### Hidden Fees That Eat Your Profits

When it comes to robo-advisors, fees can eat into your profits. According to the SEC Robo-Advisor Fee Disclosure Guidelines, the average robo-advisor fee is around 0.25%. While this may seem small, it can add up over time. For example, a $10,000 investment with a 0.25% fee would result in a loss of $25 per year.

### Tax-Loss Harvesting Showdown

Tax-loss harvesting is a feature that allows robo-advisors to sell securities at a loss to offset gains from other investments. This can help reduce your tax liability. According to the Wealthfront Tax Impact Report 2023, tax-loss harvesting can result in tax savings of up to 2.5% per year.

Here's a comparison of the tax-loss harvesting features offered by the top 5 robo-advisors:

| Robo-Advisor | Tax-Loss Harvesting | Minimum Balance Required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Schwab Intelligent Portfolios | **Yes** | **$5,000** |
| Wealthfront | **Yes** | **$10,000** |
| Betterment | **Yes** | **$10,000** |
| Vanguard Personal Advisor Services | **No** | **N/A** |
| Fidelity Go | **Yes** | **$10,000** |

## Practical Summary

Here are six concrete action bullets to help you get started with robo-advisors:

1. **Choose a robo-advisor**: Select a robo-advisor that aligns with your investment goals, risk tolerance, and financial situation.
2. **Set clear investment goals**: Determine what you want to achieve with your investments, such as saving for retirement or a down payment on a house.
3. **Regularly review and adjust your portfolio**: Ensure that your portfolio remains aligned with your goals by regularly reviewing and adjusting your investments.
4. **Consider tax-loss harvesting**: If you're eligible, consider using tax-loss harvesting to reduce your tax liability.
5. **Don't be afraid to switch**: If you find that your robo-advisor is not meeting your needs, don't be afraid to switch to a human advisor.
6. **Read the fine print**: Always read the fine print when it comes to fees, management fees, and other charges associated with your robo-advisor.
