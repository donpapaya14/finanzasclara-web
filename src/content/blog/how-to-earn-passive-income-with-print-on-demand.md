## What is Print on Demand (and Why It’s Perfect for Passive Income)
Print on demand is a business model that allows designers to sell custom products without holding any inventory. According to the **Printful 2023 Merchant Earnings Report**, part-time designers can earn between **$200-$2,000/month**. This model eliminates upfront costs, making it an attractive option for those looking to start a **zero cost side hustle**.
