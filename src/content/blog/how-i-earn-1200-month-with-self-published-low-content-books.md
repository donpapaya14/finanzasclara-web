## The One Mistake that Cost Me $500
Using copyrighted interior templates led to account suspension. To avoid this, source 100% original designs legally, as stated in the [KDP Content Guidelines 2024](https://kdp.amazon.com/help/topic/GHB3WQZG65J5).

### Avoiding Copyright Infringement Steps
1. Use original designs or purchase licenses
2. Verify template ownership before use
3. Read and follow [KDP Content Guidelines](https://kdp.amazon.com/help/topic/GHB3WQZG65J5)
