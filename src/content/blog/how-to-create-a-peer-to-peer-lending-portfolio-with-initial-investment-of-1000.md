## Creating a Diversified Lending Portfolio
To create a diversified lending portfolio, follow these steps:
1. **Diversify across loan types**: Invest in a mix of **personal loans**, **small business loans**, and **mortgage loans**.
2. **Diversify across credit grades**: Invest in loans with different credit grades, such as **A**, **B**, **C**, and **D**.
3. **Diversify across interest rates**: Invest in loans with different interest rates, such as **6%**, **8%**, and **10%**.
According to **Investopedia's 2020 Guide to P2P Lending Portfolio Management**, diversification can help reduce risk and increase returns.

## Managing and Optimizing Your Portfolio
To manage and optimize your portfolio, follow these steps:
1. **Monitor your portfolio**: Regularly review your portfolio to ensure it remains diversified.
2. **Rebalance your portfolio**: Rebalance your portfolio as needed to maintain the target allocation.
3. **Handle defaults and charge-offs**: Have a plan in place to handle defaults and charge-offs, such as **reinvesting** in new loans or **withdrawing** funds.
According to **Peer-to-Peer Finance News' 2020 Guide to P2P Lending Portfolio Optimization**, regular monitoring and rebalancing can help optimize returns and minimize risk.
