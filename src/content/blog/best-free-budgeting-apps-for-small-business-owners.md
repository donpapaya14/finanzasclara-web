3. **User-friendliness**: Is the app easy to navigate and use?

## Budgeting Best Practices for Small Business Owners
The IRS's Small Business and Self-Employed Tax Center recommends regular **financial reviews** to ensure accurate budgeting. Best practices include:
* Regularly reviewing financial statements
* Managing **cash flow** to ensure liquidity
* Planning for taxes to minimize liabilities
