## Introduction to Hidden Subscriptions
**Hidden subscriptions costing you money** can be a significant drain on your finances. According to a study by the [Federal Trade Commission (FTC)](https://www.ftc.gov), the average annual cost of hidden subscriptions is around **$300** per person. This can add up quickly, especially if you have multiple subscriptions that you are not using.

## The Average Annual Cost of Hidden Subscriptions
The average annual cost of common hidden subscriptions such as streaming services, software, and magazine subscriptions can vary greatly. A study by the [Federal Trade Commission (FTC)](https://www.ftc.gov) in 2020 found that the average person spends around **$100** per year on streaming services alone. This can increase to **$300** or more when including other types of subscriptions.

## How to Find and Cancel Hidden Subscriptions
To find and cancel hidden subscriptions, follow these steps:
1. Review your bank statements and credit card statements for recurring charges.
2. Check your online accounts for any active subscriptions.
3. Use a budgeting app to track your expenses and identify any subscriptions you may have forgotten about.
The [National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC)](https://www.nfcc.org) recommends reviewing your statements regularly to catch any unexpected charges.

## The Impact of Hidden Subscriptions on Your Budget
Hidden subscriptions can have a significant impact on your budget. According to the [Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA)](https://www.finra.org), unexpected charges can add up quickly and lead to financial stress. For example, if you have a subscription that costs **$10** per month, that can add up to **$120** per year.

## Avoiding Hidden Subscriptions in the Future
To avoid hidden subscriptions in the future, follow these tips:
1. Carefully review the terms and conditions of any subscription service before signing up.
2. Set up automatic cancellation reminders to ensure you cancel any subscriptions you no longer want.
3. Use budgeting tools to track your expenses and identify any subscriptions you may have forgotten about.
[NerdWallet](https://www.nerdwallet.com) recommends using a budgeting app to stay on top of your finances.
