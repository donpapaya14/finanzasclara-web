## Introduction to Free Brokers

For **beginner investors**, choosing the right **free brokers** is crucial to minimize costs while accessing essential tools. **Robinhood**, **Fidelity**, and **eToro** are among the top platforms offering commission-free trading, but their features and fees vary significantly. According to [Investopedia (2022)](https://www.investopedia.com), Robinhood pioneered zero-commission stock trading in 2015, while Fidelity eliminated fees in 2019. eToro, known for social trading, also offers fee-free stock and ETF trades.

Key differences:
- **Robinhood**: No account minimums, but limited research tools.
- **Fidelity**: $0 account minimum with robust educational resources.
- **eToro**: CopyTrader feature for mimicking expert investors.

## Investment Options

[The Balance (2022)](https://www.thebalance.com) compared asset availability:
- **Stocks/ETFs**: All three offer thousands of U.S. listings.
- **Options**: Robinhood and Fidelity support options trading; eToro does not.
- **Crypto**: Robinhood (18 coins) and eToro (80+ coins) outperform Fidelity’s Bitcoin-only offering.
- **Fractional Shares**: Fidelity and Robinhood allow purchases as small as $1.

**Beginner recommendation**: Start with ETFs like SPY or VTI for diversification. [A Beginner's Guide to Investing](https://www.amazon.com) explains core concepts.

## Pros and Cons of Each Broker

[Forbes (2023)](https://www.forbes.com) highlights trade-offs:

**Robinhood**
- ✅ Simple UI, instant deposits
- ❌ Limited customer support (email-only)

**Fidelity**
- ✅ 24/7 phone support, zero-expense ratio ETFs
- ❌ Cluttered interface for beginners

**eToro**
- ✅ Social trading, global market access
- ❌ High withdrawal fees ($5 per transaction)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which free broker is best for beginners?
**Fidelity** is ideal for beginners due to its educational resources and 24/7 support. Robinhood suits those prioritizing simplicity, while eToro appeals to social learners.

### Does Robinhood charge hidden fees?
Yes, Robinhood earns via **payment for order flow** (PFOF), which may increase trade costs by 0.1-0.3% per transaction (SEC, 2021).

### Can I trade options on Fidelity for free?
Fidelity charges **$0.65 per options contract** but no base commission. Robinhood offers truly free options trading.

### What’s the minimum deposit for eToro?
eToro requires a $10 minimum deposit for U.S. users and $50 for international traders.

### How do Fidelity’s ETFs compare?
Fidelity offers **four zero-expense-ratio ETFs** (e.g., FZROX), saving $3 annually per $10,000 invested versus Vanguard’s 0.03% fee.

- **Start with Fidelity** for education and low-cost ETFs.
