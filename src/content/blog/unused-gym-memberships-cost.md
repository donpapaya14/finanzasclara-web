## Introduction to Unused Gym Memberships

Unused gym membership costs drain an estimated **$1.8 billion annually** from U.S. consumers, with **67% of gym memberships going unused** according to the International Health, Racquet & Sportsclub Association (IHRSA) 2022 report. The average member pays **$58/month** ($696/year) for services they don't use, with initiation fees adding **$50-$200** upfront. This financial bleed often continues for years due to complex cancellation policies and psychological barriers.

## Why People Keep Unused Memberships

Three psychological traps prevent cancellations:
1. **Sunk cost fallacy**: "I've paid this much already" (Journal of Consumer Research 2018)
2. **Cancellation friction**: 73% of contracts require in-person or certified mail notice (FTC 2022)
3. **Optimism bias**: 89% believe they'll "start next month" (American Psychological Association 2021)

## How to Cancel a Gym Membership

Follow this FTC-approved (2022) 5-step process:

1. **Review contract**: Locate cancellation clause (typically page 3-5)
2. **Submit written notice**: Use this template:
   > "[Date]\n[Your Name]\n[Your Address]\n\nTo [Gym Name]:\nI hereby cancel my membership (#______) effective immediately per section [X] of our agreement.\nSincerely,\n[Your Name]"
3. **Send certified mail**: $4.95 at USPS with return receipt
4. **Follow up**: Call 3 days later to confirm processing
5. **Dispute charges**: If billed after cancellation, file with your bank (Regulation E protection)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does the average person waste on unused gym memberships?
The average member wastes **$58/month** ($696/year) according to IHRSA 2022 data. Over 5 years, this compounds to $3,525 with fees included.

### Can I cancel a gym membership anytime?
Only **17%** of gyms allow instant cancellation (FTC 2022). Most require 30-day notice and written documentation—often via certified mail.

### What's the cheapest way to get fit without a membership?
The [ Fitness Blender Workout DVD en Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=+Fitness+Blender+Workout+DVD&tag=vds96-20) provides studio-quality routines for $0.30/session. Public parks with fitness equipment offer 100% free options in 89% of U.S. cities (NRPA 2021).

### Do gyms make most money from unused memberships?
Yes. Gyms operate on the "**80/20 rule**"—80% of revenue comes from the 60-70% of members who rarely attend (IHRSA 2022).

### How long do most people keep unused memberships?
The median duration is **14 months** after last visit (Journal of Consumer Research 2018), with 29% paying >2 years without use.
