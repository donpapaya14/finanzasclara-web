Ignoring **asset allocation** cost me $50,000 in 2022. This loss was primarily due to overconcentration in tech stocks, specifically the ARKK ETF, which saw a **35.8%** drop in value according to the [ARK Innovation ETF (ARKK) 2022 Annual Report](https://www.ark-funds.com).

## Sector Correlation Trap
Supposedly 'diverse' tech holdings, including SaaS, fintech, and EVs, all moved together when macroeconomic conditions changed. This is evident from the [JP Morgan 2022 Correlation Analysis](https://www.jpmorgan.com), which highlights the importance of true diversification.

## The Rebalancing Opportunity Missed
Specific dates in early 2022, such as January 10th and February 14th, presented opportunities for rebalancing to lock in gains and reduce exposure. The [Morningstar Rebalancing Study 2023](https://www.morningstar.com) provides insights into the benefits of regular portfolio rebalancing.

## Psychological Damage of Concentrated Losses
The steep drop in my portfolio led to panic selling at the bottom, a common mistake among investors. According to the [Dalbar Quantitative Analysis of Investor Behavior 2023](https://www.dalbar.com), staying the course with a balanced portfolio is crucial for long-term success.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is asset allocation?
Asset allocation is the process of dividing investments among different asset classes to achieve a desired risk-return profile. According to [Investopedia](https://www.investopedia.com), it's a key component of investment strategy.

### How often should I rebalance my portfolio?
Rebalancing should be done regularly, ideally every 6-12 months, or when the portfolio drifts significantly from the target allocation. The [Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)](https://www.sec.gov) provides guidance on portfolio rebalancing.

### What are the benefits of diversification?
Diversification reduces risk by spreading investments across different asset classes, sectors, and geographic regions. A study by [Harvard Business Review](https://hbr.org) found that diversified portfolios outperform concentrated ones over the long term.

### Can I use ETFs for asset allocation?
Yes, ETFs are a popular choice for asset allocation due to their diversification benefits, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. [The Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA)](https://www.finra.org) offers resources on using ETFs in investment strategies.

### How do I avoid emotional decisions in investing?
Avoiding emotional decisions involves setting a long-term investment plan, diversifying your portfolio, and avoiding frequent checking of investment accounts. [The American Psychological Association (APA)](https://www.apa.org) discusses the psychology of investing and how to make rational decisions.

### What books should I read for investing knowledge?
Recommended books include [The Bogleheads' Guide to the Three-Fund Portfolio](https://www.bogleheads.org) and [A Random Walk Down Wall Street](https://www.amazon.com). These resources provide foundational knowledge for investing and asset allocation.
