## What are Robo-Advisors and How Do They Work?
Robo-advisors are **automated investment platforms** that use algorithms to manage investment portfolios. They differ from traditional financial advisors in that they are **low-cost** and **efficient**. A study by [The Financial Diet](https://thefinancialdiet.com/) found that robo-advisors can save investors up to **50%** in fees compared to traditional financial advisors.

## Risks and Fees Associated with Robo-Advisors
While robo-advisors offer many benefits, there are also **risks and fees** associated with them. According to [Charles Schwab](https://www.schwab.com/), management fees can range from **0.15% to 0.50%** per year. Additionally, some robo-advisors may charge **withdrawal penalties** for early withdrawals.
