## Introduction to Renting Your Parking Space for Passive Income
Renting your parking space can generate a significant **passive income**. According to a [Neighbor 2023 Market Report](https://www.neighbor.com), the average parking space owner can earn up to **$200-$300 per month**. To get started, you'll need to list your parking space on a reputable platform, such as SpotHero, Neighbor, or Pavemint.

## How to Price Competitively Without Leaving Money on the Table
To determine the optimal price for your parking space, use a formula that takes into account **Zillow parking data** and **local event calendars**. For instance, you can charge **2x the normal rate** during stadium events. ## The Legal Fine Print 90% of Owners Miss
Before renting out your parking space, make sure to review your **HOA restrictions** and **tax implications**. You'll need to report your earnings on a **1099 form** and may need to add **liability coverage** to your homeowners policy. According to the [IRS Publication 925 (2023)](https://www.irs.gov), you can deduct **$12 per year** for liability coverage.
