## The $5/day strategy that compounds fastest
Investing $5/day in SPDR ETFs can lead to significant returns. A [Vanguard](https://www.vanguard.com/) study found that daily investments outperform weekly or monthly deposits. Use a compound interest calculator to see the impact of frequent investments.

## Hidden fees that eat your returns (and how to avoid)
Expense ratios, withdrawal fees, and inactivity charges can eat into your returns. According to the [SEC](https://www.sec.gov/), these fees can range from 0.05% to 1.00% per year. For example, a $1,000 balance with a 0.50% expense ratio would incur a $5 fee per year.
