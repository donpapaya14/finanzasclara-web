## Introduction to Affiliate Marketing for Mobile Apps
Creating a mobile app for **passive income through affiliate marketing** can be a lucrative venture. ## Choosing the Right Affiliate Program for Your Mobile App
When selecting an affiliate program, consider **commission rates** and **requirements**. Top affiliate programs for mobile apps include Amazon Associates, ShareASale, and ClickBank. 

| Affiliate Program | Commission Rate | Requirements |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Amazon Associates | up to 10% | website or mobile app |
| ShareASale | up to 20% | website or mobile app |
| ClickBank | up to 75% | website or mobile app |

Affiliate marketing for mobile apps involves promoting products or services within your mobile app and earning a commission for each sale made through your unique referral link. 
### How do I choose the right affiliate program for my mobile app?
Choose an affiliate program that aligns with your mobile app's niche and target audience, and consider factors such as commission rates and requirements. 
### What are the benefits of affiliate marketing for mobile apps?
Benefits of affiliate marketing for mobile apps include **passive income**, **low risk**, and **high potential for scalability**.

Design your mobile app with call-to-actions and content that resonates with your target audience, and optimize for user experience.
